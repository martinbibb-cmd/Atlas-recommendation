## MVP educational content contracts

- Content is authored against canonical concept IDs from the educational taxonomy.
- Content entries are reusable across print, portal, QR deep dives, and optional appendix surfaces.
- Content explains Atlas truths only and does not decide recommendations.
- Analogy options are swappable and each concept also includes a no-analogy factual option.
- Print summary is mandatory for MVP entries.
- Dangerous oversimplification is mandatory for MVP entries.
