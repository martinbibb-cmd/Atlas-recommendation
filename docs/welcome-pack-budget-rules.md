# Welcome Pack Budget Rules

Welcome-pack budget rules limit page count and cognitive load before content is rendered.

## Principles

- The pack should be small enough to read.
- Every included concept needs a reason.
- QR is for depth, not as a substitute for the core print pack.
- The print pack is not a full manual.
- The technical appendix is optional or targeted.

## Budget behaviour

- never omit applicable must-print safety items
- keep the calm summary small
- cap the number of relevant explainers
- move high cognitive-load content to the appendix unless it is explicitly requested
- QR-link deep dives rather than printing everything
- prefer print-ready or static-fallback assets for print-first users
- preserve routing reasons when content is selected, deferred, moved, or omitted

## Budget outputs

- selected within budget
- deferred to QR
- moved to appendix
- omitted with reason
- budget warnings
