# Printable Welcome Pack Skeleton

## Scope

- The renderer consumes `WelcomePackPlanV1` output and mirrors the plan structure.
- It does not select concepts, alter recommendations, or infer new facts.
- The output is a print-ready skeleton with placeholder copy only.

## What it is (and is not)

- This is **not** final customer prose.
- This is **not** PDF generation.
- This is a deterministic skeleton layer for layout, sectioning, and planned asset placeholders.

## Compact print intent

- Keep the print pack compact and calm.
- Prioritise short sections, large headings, and left-aligned content.
- Must-print safety concepts are grouped into `safety_and_compliance`.

## QR depth strategy

- Deferred items remain deferred and are listed in a dedicated QR/deeper-detail section.
- QR expands depth; it does not replace core explanation in the printed pack.
- Omission/defer reasons are preserved from the composer plan.
